Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Here is how we handle your data...

At Patient Tracker, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you provide to us directly, including but not limited to your name, contact details, medical history, and any other information relevant to your healthcare. We may also collect technical information, such as your IP address, browser type, and device information, to improve our services and provide you with a better user experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your personal information is used to provide, manage, and improve our patient tracking services. This includes scheduling appointments, sending reminders, and delivering personalized healthcare information. We may also use your data for internal purposes such as analytics and service enhancement.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Our systems are designed to safeguard your data and ensure its confidentiality.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your data with healthcare providers, partners, or service providers only to the extent necessary to fulfill the services we offer and comply with applicable legal requirements. All such parties are required to uphold the same standards of data protection.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. You can update your information directly through our platform or contact our support team for assistance. We strive to honor your requests promptly, in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any significant changes and encourage you to review this policy periodically.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at support@patienttracker.com. We are here to assist you and address any inquiries you may have.
