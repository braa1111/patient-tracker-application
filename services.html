Services

At Patient Tracker, we offer a comprehensive suite of services designed to streamline and enhance your healthcare experience. Our solutions are tailored to meet your needs, from managing medications and tracking lab results to ensuring seamless communication with your healthcare providers. Explore our specialized systems, including our Pharmacy System, Laboratory System, and Communication System, each crafted to offer you convenience, efficiency, and peace of mind. Discover how our services can simplify your health management and support your journey towards better health.

The Pharmacy System is designed to ensure that you never miss a dose and that your medication management is as simple and stress-free as possible.

Pharmacy System

Our Pharmacy System integrates seamlessly with your health records to provide comprehensive medication management. With this system, you can:

  • View your current and past prescriptions with ease.
  • Request medication refills directly from the system.
  • Track your medication history and receive reminders for upcoming doses.
  • Get alerts for potential drug interactions and access detailed medication information.
  • Manage your pharmacy preferences and contact your pharmacy directly from the system.
